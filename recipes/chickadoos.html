Chickadoos

Description

This is the best dish you're ever going to have, simply put it's wonderful. I don't remember the first time I had them, but I've had them tons of times since. I can no longer imagine a time without them.

This meal can feed 4, or just a really hungry person twice. As always you can ingore the serving size.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Chickadoos
  • 1 Oven
  • About 15 minutes of time
  • A trusty plate to hold the food

Steps

  1. Turn the oven too 425f, no need to preheat.
  2. Open the bag of Chickadoos and pour it onto a baking sheet
  3. Put the sheet into the oven for 15 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
